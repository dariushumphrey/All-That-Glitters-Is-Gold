<size=48>Enemies</size>
- Minor enemy names have changed:
	testEnemy(_Melee, _Range, _Charge) -> <b>Immature (Melee, Range, Charge)</b>
- Major enemy names have changed:
	testEnemy(_Pounce, _Jump) -> <b>Mature (Pounce, Jumper)</b>
- Boss enemy names have changed:
	testEnemyBoss(_Charge, Melee) -> <b>Replevin (Charger, Gatherer, Ambuscade, Keystone)</b>
- Immature Range Enemies now strafe to their sides, move forward or backward while in combat. They can now move and shoot simultaneously. Ranged attacks can fire a burst of two (by difficulty, up to 7) projectiles with variable cooldowns between volleys.
- Immature Melee Enemies now encircle the Player for a set time before attacking.
- Non-berth Enemies' eyes now glow white when actively attacking as a visual tell.
- Immature Horde enemy max Health increased to 1000 (from 500).
- Replevin Charger, Gatherer graft colors now match their Immature attack type counterparts. Replevin Charger, Gatherer max Health increased to 50,000 (up from 40,000). 
- Max Health increases by 50% per difficulty (up from 30%).
- All bosses' damage increased to 330 (up from 220). All bosses' damage increased by 30% per difficulty (up from 25%).
- Replevin Gatherer boss now commits a melee attack for five seconds after throwing its Lucent cluster, afterwards resetting its state to gather another cluster.
