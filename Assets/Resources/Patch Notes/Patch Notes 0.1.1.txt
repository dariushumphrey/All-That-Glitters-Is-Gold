<size=48>Player</size>
- Slope traversal travel time has been reduced, and proper upward, downward force is applied dependent on orientation. 
- Player movement speed decreased to 100 (down from 150)
<size=48>Weapons</size>
- The Semi-fire Rifle now fires one round at a time with a short delay. 
- Each Weapon behavior type now has a unique reticle:
	Shotguns have a Circle reticle. 
	Full Fire Weapons have an Arrow reticle.
	Single Fire Weapons have a Cross reticle.
	Semi-Fire Weapons have a Dot reticle.
- The Machine Gun has been nerfed:
	Base damage is now 275 (down from 400). 
	Fire Rate is now 0.1 (up from 0.07)
	Magazine size is now 100 (down from 150). 
	Reserve size is now 720 (down from 840).
- The Shotgun has been buffed:
	Base damage is now 300 (up from 225)
	Spread has decreased to 0.4 (down from 0.65)
	Effective range is now 8 (down from 15)
	Maximum range is now 15 (down from 20)
- Semi-fire Rifle reserves is now 282 (down from 288)
- Statistical Cheats have been changed: 
	Hasty Hands - Increases Reload by 15%
	Hastier Hands - Increases Reload by 25%
	Far Sight - Increases Effective Range by 10% of maximum Range
	Farther Sight - Increases Effective Range by 20% of maximum Range
	Deep Yield - Increases active Mag by 12%
	Deeper Yield - Increases active Mag by 24%
	Deep Stores - Increases Reserve ammo by 15%
	Deeper Stores - Increases Reserve ammo by 30%
- Statistical Cheats no longer negatively impact a Weapon’s performance.
- Pay to Win (Exotic Machine Gun) 
	No longer creates stacks if the Player’s Lucent funds are below asking price.
<size=48>Game</size>
- The text that displays a Player’s numerical Health no longer cuts off on 1920 x 1080 (or similar) displays.
- The Kiosk’s purchase price has been decreased to 5,000 (down from 10,000). Its asking price now increases on Difficulty 2 by 100%, up to 500% on Difficulty 5. It no longer creates a higher rarity Weapon if the Player’s funds are zero.
