<size=48>Player</size>
- <color=yellow>[Implement]</color> The Inventory now has a sorting type that changes between "All" and "Favorite" using the 'Up' arrow.
- Shield now recharges by 1% of max Shield. 
- <color=cyan>[FIX]</color> Fixed an issue where Shield recharges were inconsistent in builds.  
- <color=cyan>[FIX]</color> Fixed an issue where moving, evading diagonally would yield farther traversal than moving in single directions.
<size=48>Weapons</size>
- <color=yellow>[Implement]</color> Weapons can be favorited using the 'F' key on the Inventory page (Main Menu, in-game).
- Weapons can no longer be switched during reloads.
- <color=yellow>[Implement]</color> Weapons roll with a new RNG element: Platforms
	- Default: Leaves base Weapon performance unchanged.
	- Efficient: Increases damage, zoom, aim assist, reduces fire rate, recoil.
	- Chatter: Reduces damage, aim assist, zoom, increases fire rate, recoil.
	- Tempered: Increases damage, fire rate, zoom, aim assist, reduces recoil.
	- Siphonic: Adds 1% of Health, Shield on hits, 15% on Melee kills.
	- Mining: Spawns, shatters a Lucent cluster on weapon impacts.
	- Trenchant: Weapon hits apply Health debuffs.
		Melee hits apply Damage Over Time.
		Evading applies Slowed.
	- Cache: Regenerates +1 Grenade charge every 2s.
- Weapon Rarity changes: 
	- Rarity 1: Platforms only.
	- Rarity 2: Platforms + Stat Cheats.
	- Rarity 3: Platforms + Stat Cheats + 1 Functional Cheat.
	- Rarity 4, 5: Platforms + Stat Cheats + 2 Functional Cheats.
- <color=yellow>[Implement]</color> Added new Weapon type: Grenade Launcher
- <color=yellow>[Implement]</color> Added new Functional Cheat: Activator Drone
	- Selects targets at random or through manual aim to attack
	- Attacks trigger passive-based Platforms.
	- Its weapon launches rockets on Cache platform weapons.
	- (Fated) Attacks activate "Wait! Now I'm Ready!", Efficacy
	Inoculated, Cadence, and Enshroud.
- <color=yellow>[Implement]</color> Added new Exotic Functional Cheat: Flashpoint (Exotic Grenade Launcher)
	- Fires floating Lucent mines for manual detonation.
	- Switching Weapons leaves mines intact.
	- Companion Cheat is Positive-Negative.
-Improved Superweapon Firing VFX (Apathetic - Exotic Pistol).
- <color=cyan>[FIX]</color> Fixed an issue where Weapons would subtract from reserves instead of their magazines when firing immediately after auto-reloads.
- <color=cyan>[FIX]</color> Fixed an issue where Forager (Fated) was activating on Underfoot (Exotic SMG).
- Mercies (Exotic Semi Fire Rifle) 
	Player Camera vertical clamp increased to 89.
<size=48>Enemies</size>
- <color=yellow>[Implement]</color> Combatants now possess additional identifiers:
	- Melee enemies now use appendages that extend/retract during attacks.
	- Range, Jump enemies, Replevin Keystone boss now use mandibles that open/close during attacks.
	- Pounce enemies' front graft colors take after Lucent cluster colors.
-Replevin Gatherer fight has been improved: 
	-Now throws full Lucent crystals instead of clusters, requiring multiple hits to shatter.
	-Gathers its crystals by jumping through the ceiling, returning to the arena after collection.
	-Shooting the crystal slightly delays the throw timer.
	- <color=cyan>[FIX]</color> Fixed an issue where their attack window could be reset perpetually by approaching, retreating from the boss.
- <color=cyan>[FIX]</color> Fixed an issue where Replevin Charger boss refused to attack while living enemies remained on higher floors.
- Replevin Ambuscade now prevents other crystals from priming if another crystal is already primed.
